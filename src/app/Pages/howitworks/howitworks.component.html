How Voice First Works

Voice First simplifies issue tracking and resolution with an intelligent, automated workflow. Here’s how it works:

1
Instant Issue Reporting

Issues can be reported by employees, customers, or inspectors through voice command, mobile app input, text, or by uploading photos and videos. The system automatically captures essential details, including a description, GPS location, and attachments for verification.

2
AI-Powered Intelligent Logging

Once reported, the issue is logged into VoiceFirst’s system. If submitted via voice, AI transcribes the speech and extracts key details. Every report is time-stamped and, when applicable, geotagged. The system categorizes issues by type, department, and severity, either automatically or through preset filters.

3
Real-Time Notifications & Assignment

Relevant personnel receive immediate alerts via in-app notifications, SMS, email, or WhatsApp. Unlike traditional systems that only log issues for later review, VoiceFirst proactively pushes notifications, ensuring timely action.

4
Fast & Efficient Resolution

Assigned team members update the issue status such as Work in Progress, Done, or Closed; along with resolution notes and supporting evidence like photos or videos. If an issue is not addressed within a predefined timeframe, escalation rules trigger notifications to higher management for further action.

5
Tracking & Verification

Managers can monitor all open issues through a live dashboard, tracking who is handling them and their current status. Verified inputs, such as GPS location and document uploads, ensure data accuracy and reliability.

6
Automated Reports & Actionable Insights

VoiceFirst consolidates data into meaningful insights, allowing users to generate custom reports on demand or receive automated daily and weekly performance summaries. Businesses can identify trends, prevent recurring issues, and improve operational efficiency through data-driven decision-making.
