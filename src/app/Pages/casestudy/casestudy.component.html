Case Study 2 : How a Municipal Government Enhanced Citizen Issue Resolution

Industry : Government & Public Services

Challenge : A city’s local government office struggled with tracking and responding to citizen complaints about infrastructure, public spaces, and facilities. The existing system was slow, leading to delays in issue resolution and citizen dissatisfaction.

Solution : Voice First was deployed across municipal departments, enabling field officers and citizens to report issues via mobile voice input, text, and image uploads. Each report was geotagged and routed to the correct department for immediate action.

Results:

  • Citizen complaint resolution time reduced by 50%
  • Enhanced transparency with automated tracking & reporting
  • Increased public trust and engagement
Case Study 3 : Reducing Downtime in Manufacturing with Voice First

Industry : Manufacturing & Industrial Services

Results:
