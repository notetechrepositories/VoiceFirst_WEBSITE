Frequently Asked Questions

  • How does VoiceFirst ensure data security and privacy?

    Security is our top priority. VoiceFirst employs end-to-end encryption to protect all data, ensuring that sensitive information remains secure. We implement role-based access controls (RBAC) to restrict system permissions based on user roles, preventing unauthorized access. VoiceFirst is also designed to comply with data protection regulations like GDPR and HIPAA, safeguarding user privacy at every level.

  • Can VoiceFirst integrate with our existing systems?

    Yes, VoiceFirst offers seamless integration with your existing software ecosystem. Our robust API allows for direct connectivity with CRM systems (Salesforce, HubSpot), IT service management tools (ServiceNow, Zendesk), ERP platforms (SAP, Oracle), and enterprise communication tools (Slack, Microsoft Teams). This ensures a smooth workflow without disrupting your current operations.

  • Who can submit issues into VoiceFirst?

    VoiceFirst provides flexible user roles, allowing businesses to define who can submit issues. Depending on your setup, reports can be submitted by employees, QA inspectors, vendors, clients, or even the general public—all with customized access permissions to maintain control and security.

  • Do I need special devices for voice reporting?

    No. VoiceFirst works with any standard smartphone or computer microphone—there’s no need for proprietary hardware. Users can report issues hands-free using voice commands, making it a versatile solution for field workers, customer support teams, and businesses of all sizes.

  • Can VoiceFirst be accessed from mobile devices?

    Yes! VoiceFirst is fully mobile-optimized and accessible via Android and iOS apps as well as web browsers, allowing users to report and track issues from anywhere, anytime.

  • How are reported issues assigned and tracked?

    Issues are automatically categorized, prioritized, and assigned based on predefined workflows. Managers can track the status, resolution progress, and escalations through a real-time dashboard with notifications via email, WhatsApp, and in-app alerts.

  • Does VoiceFirst support multiple languages?

  • Can VoiceFirst generate reports and analytics?

  • What happens if an issue is not resolved on time?
